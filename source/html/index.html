Introduction

In Chicago Restaurant Week, hundreds of restaurants will feature special prix fixe menus starting at $22 for brunch and lunch, and $33 and/or $44 for dinner (excluding beverages, tax and gratuity).

Schedule

The 11th Annual Chicago Restaurant Week returns Jan. 26 - Feb. 8, 2018. Enjoy dining deals throughout the city and suburbs and experience Chicago's rich, culinary diversity.
